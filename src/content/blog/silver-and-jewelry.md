## Quality and Care Tips for Silver Jewelry

When buying **silver and jewelry** items, always make sure you’re getting genuine quality. The best silver pieces are made of sterling silver – 92.5% pure silver, often stamped with a **925** hallmark. This mark (or the word "sterling") is your assurance that the piece is authentic and will stand the test of time. Once you have quality silver jewelry, taking care of it will keep it shining.

Store your silver pieces in a cool, dry place (ideally in a soft pouch or lined jewelry box) to minimize tarnish. It’s fine to keep silver and [gold jewelry](/blog/gold-jewellery-edmonton/) together, but try to give each piece its own space or pouch so they don’t scratch each other. You can also occasionally polish your silver gently with a soft cloth to remove any tarnish and maintain its brilliance.

## Can You Mix Silver and Gold Jewelry? Absolutely!

![Silver and Jewelry](/assets/images/blog/wp-import/2025/09/Can-You-Mix-Silver-and-Gold-Jewelry-1024x536.webp)

One common question we hear is whether you can wear silver and gold jewelry together. The answer is absolutely yes – mixing metals is a modern fashion trend. The key is to make it look intentional and balanced. For example, you might layer a couple of silver and gold necklaces together, or stack some sterling silver bangles alongside a few gold bracelets for a chic contrast.

You could even wear a two-tone piece (jewelry that combines both silver and gold) to tie the look together. Mixing silver and gold allows you to create a personalized style, so don’t hesitate to experiment with your favorite pieces.

### How can I spot fake jewelry?

Look for proper markings: sterling silver has a "925" stamp and real gold jewelry has karat marks (10K, 14K, etc.). Real gold and silver are not magnetic (if a piece sticks to a magnet, it may be fake), and genuine pieces usually feel solid and well-crafted. When in doubt, buy from a reputable jeweller or get an appraisal.

### Is it better to buy jewelry at a jewelry store or a department store?

A dedicated jewelry store is generally better for important purchases. Jewelry stores offer a wider selection and knowledgeable service. You’re more likely to find unique, high-quality pieces with personalized care at a jeweller, whereas department stores have a very limited fine jewelry selection.
