### **How Rough Stones Become the Diamonds You Fall in Love With**

![Artisan polishing diamond at diamond jewellery shop in Surrey](/assets/images/blog/wp-import/2025/05/20250527_1239_Artisan-Diamond-Craftsmanship_simple_compose_01jw8932z9f81aq2q135f0v77e-1-1024x683.webp)

A diamond’s journey begins far from display lights and velvet trays. Deep underground, the stone forms under incredible heat and pressure. We make sure the mines that supply us operate ethically, because the sparkle means little if someone was harmed bringing it to you.

Once a rough crystal arrives in the workshop, an experienced cutter studies its angles the way a chef inspects a fine truffle. One wrong facet and the fire is gone. Cutters coax classics like the round brilliant and the princess cut out of those unpolished chunks, then polish each facet until it throws light across the room.

Finally, an independent lab—often the [**GIA (Gemological Institute of America)**](https://www.gia.edu/)—grades the stone for Cut, Clarity, Colour and Carat. That report slips into the envelope we hand you with every purchase, so you always know exactly what rests in your ring box.

### **Why Diamond Jewellery Is So Valuable: A Look at Diamond Types and Their Deeper Meaning**

People visit a **jewellery store** for many things, but diamonds are still the first choice for life-changing moments. Here’s why:

-   **Strength.** They’re the hardest natural material on earth.

-   **Individuality.** No two stones share the exact same inclusions or colour shading.

-   **Legacy.** A well-cared-for diamond often outlives its first owner and becomes part of the family.


Today you can choose among three main kinds:

1.  **Natural diamonds** – classic beauty, mined from the earth.

2.  **Lab-grown diamonds** – identical sparkle but easier on both price and planet.

3.  **Fancy-coloured diamonds** – yellows, pinks, and blues that turn heads instantly.


For more background on each type, the **[De Beers Diamond Education](https://www.debeers.com/en-us/education.html)** page offers a friendly deep dive.
