![Gold Jewelry for Men](/assets/images/blog/wp-import/2025/09/Understanding-Gold-Purity-1024x561.webp)

When buying **Everyday Gold Jewellery in Canada**, purity plays a key role in both price and durability:

-   **22K Gold (91.6%)** – Rich yellow tone, beautiful for chains, earrings, and bangles.
-   **24K Gold (99.9%)** – Purest form of gold, ideal for investment pieces, coins, and select pendants.

## **5\. How to Care for Your Everyday Gold Jewellery**

![Everyday Gold Jewellery Canada](https://jdschoolofdesign.b-cdn.net/wp-content/uploads/2024/06/The-Timeless-Allure-of-Gold-Jewellery-Design-Trends-techniques-and-tradition-1.jpg)

Gold is durable but still needs care to stay shiny and scratch-free. Follow these quick tips:

1.  Remove jewellery before swimming, gym, or shower.
2.  Keep it in a soft pouch or separate box.
3.  Wipe gently with a soft cloth after use.
4.  Clean monthly with mild soap and warm water.

These small steps will help your **Everyday Gold Jewellery Canada** collection stay beautiful year after year.
