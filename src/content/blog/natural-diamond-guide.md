## What Are Natural Diamonds?

Natural-diamonds are formed naturally under the Earth's surface through intense heat and pressure. They’re not made in a lab, but instead discovered and mined after millions of years of formation. That’s why they’re often called real or mined diamonds.

When you wear a natural-diamond, you’re wearing a true gift from nature.
