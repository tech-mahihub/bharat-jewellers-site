### What Does "Pure Gold" Really Mean?

![pure gold](/assets/images/blog/wp-import/2025/06/gold-ring-purity-1-1024x683.webp)

When people say **pure gold**, they’re usually talking about **24 karat gold**, which is **99.9% pure**. That’s as pure as gold gets — it’s soft, shiny, and beautiful. But here’s something most people don’t know: 24K gold is **too soft** to be turned into jewellery you can actually wear every day.

That’s why most jewellery is made from **22K gold**, which is still very pure — [about **91.6% gold**](https://www.gold.org/goldhub/data) — but strong enough to hold designs and last longer. So when you’re buying **[pure gold jewellery](https://www.gold.org/about-gold/about-gold-jewellery)**, especially in Indian designs, you’re most likely buying 22K, not 24K — and that’s perfectly fine.

### How to Check Gold Purity Before You Buy

![pure gold](/assets/images/blog/wp-import/2025/06/gold-purity-checker-1-1024x683.webp)

Now that you know about gold types, how can you be sure the piece you’re holding is actually **pure gold**? Here are a few simple tips you can trust:

#### 1\. **Check for the BIS Hallmark**

This is the easiest and most reliable way to know your gold’s purity. A **BIS hallmark** means the gold has been checked and approved. You’ll usually see:

-   The karat (like 22K)
-   A BIS stamp
-   A code for the jeweller
-   The year it was certified

Even though we’re based in Canada, **Bharat Jewellers** still follows Indian BIS standards so you get the same level of purity and trust.

#### 2\. **Ask for a Purity Certificate**

A genuine jeweller will always give you a certificate along with your jewellery. It mentions the gold’s weight, karat, and purity — and it’s super useful if you ever want to sell or upgrade your piece later.

#### 3\. **Use a Purity Machine (If Available)**

Some jewellers, like us, have machines that test the purity of gold right in front of you. It’s fast, easy, and reassuring — especially when you’re buying something expensive like **pure gold earrings** or a bridal necklace.
