## How to Identify Authentic Indian Jewelry in Surrey

![Indian Jewelry in Surrey](/assets/images/blog/wp-import/2026/02/How-to-Identify-Authentic-Indian-Jewelry-in-Surrey-1024x536.webp)

### ✔ Hallmark Certification

Always check for:

-   **[BIS Hallmark](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/BIS_hallmark)**
-   **Purity mark** (22K – 916 or 24K – 999)
-   **Jeweller’s stamp**
-   **Certificate of authenticity**

These marks prove the gold is real.

### ✔ [Gold Purity](/blog/pure-gold/) Explained

**22K Gold (91.6%)**

-   Best for daily wear
-   Strong and long-lasting
-   Popular for bangles, chains, and earrings

**24K Gold (99.9%)**

-   Purest gold
-   Bright yellow color
-   Best for investment and special occasions
