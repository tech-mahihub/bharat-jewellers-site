Our Heritage

A journey of trust, spanning cities and generations.

1985 — A Trusted Family Jeweller

Our story begins with a small, family-owned shop built on the foundations of trust and exceptional craftsmanship.

2005 — Expansion to Surrey

We opened our first flagship store in Surrey, BC, bringing our legacy of fine jewellery to a new community.

2015 — Calgary Store Opens

Our journey continued eastward with a new location in Calgary, Alberta, sharing our passion for exquisite design.

2025 — Edmonton's New Destination

We proudly opened our doors in Edmonton, becoming a premier destination for luxury jewellery in the heart of Alberta.
